Done with A/L, what's next?

If you are trying to select the best option after the Advanced Level Examination, let us guide you.

Looking to study abroad?

We connect Sri Lankan students who are seeking higher education abroad with undergraduates and graduates from respective universities, who would guide them through the process.

Looking for Professional / Vocational Qualifications?

We can guide you on Professional and Vocational qualifications required to excel in your career path.​

Looking for a scholarship?

There are so many scholarships out there. We can advise you on how to grab these golden opportunities.

Willing to go that 'Extra Mile' with your research?

Submitting a paper for an International Journal / Conference is not easy. Join our Research Collaboration programme to find a qualified, experienced mentor, whose expertise will take you there.

Moving to Sri Lanka?

Planning to return to your motherland? Seeking job opportunities in Sri Lanka? We provide guidance and information for everyone who want to return or migrate to Sri Lanka
